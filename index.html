About Cyn

Movement is a practice, not a performance

I'm a certified freelance Pilates instructor who partners with studios across the area to bring focused, high-quality instruction to their communities. Whether I'm leading a group reformer class or working one-on-one in a private session, my approach stays the same: meet you where you are and help you move forward.

With a deep understanding of anatomy and alignment, I create sessions that are both challenging and deeply nourishing. Every cue, every transition, every breath is intentional.

My Approach

“I believe Pilates is for every body. My role is to create a space where you feel safe to explore movement, build strength, and reconnect with yourself.”

Every session begins with listening. Whether you're recovering from an injury, training for performance, or simply seeking more ease in daily life, the work always starts with understanding where you are today.
